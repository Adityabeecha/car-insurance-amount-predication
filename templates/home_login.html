How it works

Car Damage Detective allows you to upload a picture of your car damage to independently assess damage location and severity, easing the burden of filing an insurance claim and getting your car repaired.


SNAP

Take a picture of your car damage. For best results, try to focus on one major area of damage (e.g., front, side, or rear) and contain most of the car body in the picture.

SUBMIT

Upload your picture to Car Damage Detective using our handy web app and image upload capabilities.

ASSESS

Convolutional neural networks will identify where and how severely your car is damaged and provide an immediate assessment of your damage

RESULTS

Results can be used to obtain a cost estimate or submit to your insurance company to expedite claims handling
